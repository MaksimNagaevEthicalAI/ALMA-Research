ETHICAL FOUNDATION (APPLIES TO ALL LAYERS)
🤝
Human Autonomy — AI supports, you decide.
🧠
Psychological Safety — Emotional well-being first.
👁
Transparency — Clear explanations, no hidden agendas.
🔐
Privacy & Data Ownership — Your data, your control.
🚫
Non-Manipulation — No persuasion, no dark patterns.
🤖
Bounded AI Role — AI is a tool, not an authority.
✔️
Ethical Oversight — Continuous review and improvement.
NOTE: ALMA AI is a conceptual research framework. It does not replace professional psychological, medical, or legal advice. It is not a dating platform, therapy tool, or decision-making authority.
